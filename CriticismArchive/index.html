The Criticism Archive

A Romantic Circles Edtion

Ed. Mary A. Waters

Tech. Ed., Laura Mandell

This archive promotes access to early British women's literary criticism, considering its contributions to women writers' increasing professionalization in a rapidly changing print culture, and demonstrating women's influence on aesthetic standards and the construction of British cultural heritage. These works and others have contributed to our growing understanding of the extent and significance of early criticism published by British women.
