For the Spot where the HERMITAGE stood on St. Herbert’s
Island, Derwent-Water.
If Thou in the dear love of some one Friend1Hast been so happy, that thou know’st what thoughts2Will, sometimes, in the happiness of love3Make the heart sick, then wilt thou reverence4This quiet spot.——St. Herbert hither came,5And here, for many seasons, from the world6Remov’d, and the affections of the world,7He dwelt in solitude.—But he had left8A Fellow-labourer, whom the good Man lov’d9As his own soul.
