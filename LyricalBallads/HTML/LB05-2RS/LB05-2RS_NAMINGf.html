P O E M S

on the

NAMING OF PLACES.




ADVERTISEMENT.

By Persons resident in the country and attached to rural
objects, many places will be found unnamed or of un-
known names, where little Incidents will have occurred,
or feelings been experienced, which will have given to such
places a private and peculiar interest. From a wish to give
some sort of record to such Incidents, or renew the grati-
fication of such Feelings, Names have been given to Places
by the Author and some of his Friends, and the follow-
ing Poems written in consequence.
