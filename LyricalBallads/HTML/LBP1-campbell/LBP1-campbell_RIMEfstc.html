THE ANCYENT MARINER,

a poet’s reverie.



ARGUMENT.

How a Ship having first sailed to the Equator,
was driven by Storms to the cold Country towards the
South Pole : How the Ancient Mariner, cruelly and in con-
tempt of the Laws of Hospitality, killed a Sea-bird ; and how he was followed by many and strange Judgments ; and
in what Manner he came back to his own Country.
