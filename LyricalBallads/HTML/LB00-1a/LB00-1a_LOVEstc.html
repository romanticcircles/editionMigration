LOVE.

1

All Thoughts, all Passions, all Delights, 1Whatever stirs this mortal Frame,2All are but Ministers of Love,3 And feed his sacred flame.4

2

Oft in my waking dreams do I5Live o’er again that happy hour,6When midway on the Mount I lay7 Beside the Ruin’d Tower.8

3

The Moonshine stealing o’er the scene9Had blended with the Lights of Eve;10And she was there, my Hope, my Joy,11 My own dear Genevieve!12
